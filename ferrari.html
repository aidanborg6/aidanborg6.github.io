Ferrari 250 GTO

The 1962 Ferrari 250 GTO is one of the most iconic sports cars ever built, combining stunning design with race-winning performance. Powered by a 3.0-liter V12 engine, it produced around 296 horsepower, delivering an exhilarating driving experience with a top speed of 280 km/h (174 mph).

With only 36 units ever produced, its rarity and pedigree have made it one of the most valuable classic cars in the world. Today, the 1962 250 GTO stands as the ultimate symbol of Ferrari’s golden era.
