Goodwood Festival of Speed Adds New “Restomod Rally” Section

February 26, 2025

Goodwood Festival of Speed is embracing the future of classic car culture by debuting a new “Restomod Rally” class. Expect to see reengineered icons like the Singer Porsche 911, Eagle Speedster, and the Cyan Racing Volvo P1800 tearing up the hill climb.

The section is intended to celebrate engineering innovation while maintaining vintage aesthetics. Purists may wince, but restomod fans are thrilled to see this growing movement earn a spotlight at one of the world's most prestigious automotive festivals.
