Pebble Beach Concours d’Elegance Gears Up for a Star-Studded Return

May 2, 2025

The Pebble Beach Concours d’Elegance returns to California’s Monterey Peninsula this August, drawing in collectors, enthusiasts, and industry elites from around the globe. Among the highlights this year are a rare 1937 Bugatti Type 57SC Atlantic and a restored Ferrari 250 GT SWB California Spyder.

Organizers have announced a spotlight on American Muscle, with a featured class honoring iconic models from the 1960s and 70s. With RM Sotheby’s auctioning several high-profile classics, many are calling this the must-attend event of the year for collectors.
