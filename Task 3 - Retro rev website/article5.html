Amelia Island 2025: Top Sales and Surprises Revealed

March 15, 2025

The 2025 Amelia Island Concours delivered big numbers this March. A 1961 Aston Martin DB4 GT sold for $3.4 million, topping estimates by 15%. Meanwhile, a humble yet immaculate 1970 Datsun 240Z shocked attendees by fetching nearly $120,000—highlighting the rise of Japanese classics.

With over $45 million in total sales, Amelia has cemented itself as a bellwether for the spring market. Expect ripple effects at upcoming sales in Monterey and Paris.
