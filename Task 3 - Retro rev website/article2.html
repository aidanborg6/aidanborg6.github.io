EV Conversions Meet Resistance Among Classic Car Purists

April 27, 2025

While some hail electric vehicle (EV) conversions as a sustainable way to preserve aging classics, purists argue these conversions erase the very soul of the cars. Recent conversions of Jaguars, Porsches, and even vintage Mustangs have gone viral, sparking heated conversations across forums.

Companies like Lunaz and Electrogenic defend their work, saying they’re future-proofing beloved cars, but traditionalists insist that the sound and mechanical feel are irreplaceable. Expect this debate to keep growing as EV technology becomes more accessible.
