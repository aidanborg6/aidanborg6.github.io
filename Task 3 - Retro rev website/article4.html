Classic Car Market Sees Surprising Shift in 2025 Values

March 30, 2025

This year’s market reports show a shift in what collectors are valuing. Modern classics like the BMW E30 M3, Toyota Supra MK4, and early Porsche 911s are climbing in value, driven by younger buyers entering the space. Meanwhile, demand for 1950s American cruisers has cooled slightly.

Industry analysts cite nostalgia cycles and generational preferences as the primary reasons. Auction houses are adjusting catalogs accordingly, and many predict this is just the beginning of a larger generational handover in classic car collecting.
